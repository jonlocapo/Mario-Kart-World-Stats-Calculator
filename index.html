Mario Kart World — Stats Calculator

Pick a character & vehicle to see combined stats, explore the Pareto frontier of any two stats, and search for builds that beat yours. Pure HTML/JS, no dependencies. Data: community stat rips (see bottom).

2 · Pareto Frontier (two stats)

The frontier = builds where you can't improve one axis without giving up the other. E.g. set X = Speed, Y = Acceleration to read off the best mini-turbo available at each speed. Both axes treated as "higher is better".

3 · Combo Finder — beat your build

Choose a base build, then say which stats must improve (>) or at least stay equal (≥). Optionally require a true upgrade (never worse in anything).

4 · Reference tables

Character stat groups (click to expand)
Vehicle stat groups (click to expand)

Characters and vehicles that share a stat line are listed together on one row. Speed & Handling each split into three terrain types: Road (solid), Off-Road (coarse/rough), Water (liquid). A build's stat = character value + vehicle value. Accel also governs mini-turbo; Weight also affects coin capacity.
